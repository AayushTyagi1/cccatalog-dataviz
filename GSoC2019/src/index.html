The Big Picture

There is no larger compendium of shared human knowledge and creativity than the Commons, including over 1.4 billion digital works available under CC tools. This graph represents all the Commons community. Here you can visualize which domains have licensed content, how the use CC licenses, and to which other domains they relate too. How to interact: You can zoom in and ou using two fingers and moving them up or down in your touchpad. Click on an empty space and drag into any direction to move the graph. Click on the name of a node to see some statistics about their use of CC licenses.
